It's not working — help
The camera page never loads
Your phone and computer must be on the same network, and the RollShare Companion App must be open and running.
  • First: On your phone, go to Settings and turn Wi-Fi off, then back on. Then try the link again.
  • At home (home router): Make sure Wi-Fi is enabled in your phone's settings and that it's connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer.
  • On public Wi-Fi (hotel, school, coffee shop): Public networks often block devices from talking to each other. Turn on your phone's hotspot, connect your laptop to it, then rescan the QR code.
I don't see "RollShare DiceCam" in Zoom or Teams
Reboot your computer and try again. After a fresh install the virtual camera driver needs a full restart to register with Windows — simply restarting Zoom or Teams is usually not enough.
My phone keeps saying the connection isn't private
Follow the steps in Step 2 above to tap through the warning. Your data never leaves your local network.
I need to rescan the QR code
The QR code is shown on the RollShare Companion App window on your computer. Scan it fresh with your phone camera to get a new link.
