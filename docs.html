Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my video displaying mirrored in my video conferencing app?
Most video conferencing apps (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet) automatically mirror your camera preview on your own screen so it feels like looking in a mirror — this is normal and only affects what you see. Your participants see the correct, non-mirrored image.

If you want to confirm this, ask a participant or check the "Share screen" or "Spotlight" view. If the image truly appears mirrored for everyone, look for a Mirror my video or Flip horizontal option in your app's video settings and make sure it is turned off.
