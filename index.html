RollShare is a desk-side companion for online D&D nights. Point your phone at your dice tray and your party sees your dice, your face, and the magic… all in one video feed inside Zoom, Teams or Discord.

Your dice, on screen

Your phone becomes a dice cam that auto-pops into frame the moment you roll. No more ‘I swear it was a 20.’

💎

Show off your lucky set

We get so attached to our dice. Roll Share gives you more opportunities to actually use the dice you’ve spent years collecting — your party gets to see the obsidian d20 you’ve nat-20’d with three sessions running and witness just how cursed (or blessed) they really are.

🎭

Share the ‘no way that just happened’ moments

When the bard seduces the dragon on a nat 20 after the wizard fumbles a Feather Fall — those stories live forever. Roll Share makes sure everyone sees them happen, together.

How it works

  1. 01
    Open the companion
    Run Roll Share on the same computer you join your game on.
  2. 02
    Pair your phone
    Scan a QR code. Prop your phone over your dice tray.
  3. 03
    Pick Roll Share as your camera
    In Zoom or Discord, choose ‘Roll Share Camera’ — and play.
