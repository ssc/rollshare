📥 Download Traveller RPG Character Sheet

Works on the Roll20 editor page — keep your character sheet(s) open there. Exports the entire sheet (stats, skills, sub-skills, inventory, bio, notes…) as structured Markdown. If more than one character sheet is open, a picker lets you choose which one to download.

📥 Download Traveller RPG Character Sheet
  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B in Chrome/Edge)
  2. Open the Roll20 editor and open the character sheet(s) you want
  3. Drag this button to your bookmarks bar, then click it on the editor page

📅 Chat Archive by Date and Download Button

Works from any Roll20 campaign page. If you're not already on the chat archive, it navigates there automatically. Once on the archive, it shows timestamps and collapses everything into collapsible date sections. Safe to click multiple times (actually you have to click it twice if you're not already on the Chat Archive page).

📅 Chat Archive by Date and Download Button
  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B in Chrome/Edge)
  2. Drag the button above onto the bookmarks bar
  3. Click it on any Roll20 campaign page — it will navigate to the archive if needed, then click once more to group
How it works: On a non-archive Roll20 page → redirects to the full chat archive. Already on the archive → groups messages by date with all sections collapsed, and adds a 📥 Download .txt button to the page.
