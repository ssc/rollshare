Sign-in help

Didn't get your sign-in code?

Here's what to check.

What to expect

When you sign in to RollShare, we send a 6-digit code to the email address you entered. The email comes from:

rollshare.app@gmail.com

The code typically arrives within 30 seconds. It expires after 1 hour.

Step-by-step checklist

  1. Check your spam or junk folder.
    Email clients sometimes flag automated messages. Look for an email from rollshare.app@gmail.com with the subject "Your RollShare sign-in code" (or similar). If you find it there, mark it as "Not spam" so future codes arrive in your inbox.
  2. Check you entered the right email address.
    Go back to the sign-in screen and make sure there are no typos — an easy thing to miss on a phone keyboard.
  3. Wait a little longer and try "Resend it".
    Occasionally transactional email is briefly delayed by the receiving mail server. Wait 60 seconds, then click resend it in the sign-in dialog.
  4. Check your email provider's filters.
    Some corporate or school email accounts block external senders by default. If you're using a work address, try again with a personal Gmail, Outlook, or similar account.
  5. Add us to your contacts.
    Adding rollshare.app@gmail.com to your contacts or address book tells most mail clients to always deliver from this address. This is the most reliable long-term fix.

Still not arriving?

If you've worked through the checklist above and still haven't received the code, please open an issue on the project issue tracker and include the email address you used (or a rough description — "Gmail", "Outlook", "corporate domain") so we can investigate on our end.

RollShare is an early-stage independent project. Email delivery is currently handled through a standard Gmail account, which works well for most addresses but has occasional reliability quirks with certain mail servers. We'll move to a dedicated transactional mail provider as the project matures.
